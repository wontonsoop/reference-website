Naming Conventions

Rules for naming files and paths

  • Don't use spaces

  • Only use lowercase

  • Only letters, numbers, and dashes

Commit messages

The best practices for commit messages.

  • Minimum 3 words & 10 characters

  • Proper spelling & grammar

  • Capital starting letter, no period

  • Must Start with an imperative verb: Create, Fix, Add, Solve etc.

  • Pretend every commit starts with the phrase: "This commit will..."

HTML Elements

Headings

Heading 1 is used for the main topic, it'll be smaller than the title.

Heading 2 is for not competing with H1, it's slightly bigger than body text.

Header 3 is for a block of text that is for pieces of information not as important as h2

Here's h4, as you can see there are many headers, and they can get even smaller.

Here's h5, we are getting smaller.

Here's h6, look how small it is here!

Paragraphs

In the p tag you can add text, you can see through here you can read my writing. I'll talk about an interest for a bit to add some text. I love to listen to music in my free time, my favorite genre being Electro Swing. It's very upbeat and jazzy.

This is a song I like called Betty Boop.

Blockquotes

The blockquote tag is used for quotations, usually for longer blocks of text.

"Time is too slow for those who wait, too swift for those who fear, too long for those who grieve, too short for those who rejoice, but for those who love, time is eternity" -Henry Van Dyke

The quotation tag on the other hand is used for smaller quotes.

The only costant in life is change. -Heraclitus
